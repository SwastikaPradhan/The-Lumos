How vr can help in interior design?

Virtual Reality (VR) significantly enhances the field of interior design by offering immersive experiences
and practical benefits. Designers and clients can visualize spaces in 3D, enabling better decision-making. VR
allows for real-time design modifications, streamlining the feedback and revision process. It also aids in testing
various color schemes, furniture layouts, and materials before implementation. Clients can experience their future
spaces firsthand, fostering a deeper connection and confidence in design choices. Collaboration among stakeholders becomes more efficient, regardless of physical distance. VR ultimately saves time and resources by
reducing the need for physical prototypes and costly revisions, leading to more accurate and satisfying interior design outcomes.
